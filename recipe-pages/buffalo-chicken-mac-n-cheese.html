Buffalo Chicken Mac n' Cheese

Description

Yummy Mac n' Cheese chock full of protein and delicious cheese!

Ingredients

Serves 6

  • 336 g Elbow Pasta
  • 360g Fat Free Cottage Cheese
  • 150g 1/3 Fat Cream Cheese
  • 15g Cheddar Powder
  • 15g Ranch Seasoning
  • 6 Laughing Cow Cheese Wedges
  • 90g Buffalo Sauce
  • 18oz Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • Seasoning (Smoked Paprika, Ancho Chilli Powder, Onion Powder, Garlic Powder, Salt, Pepper

Directions

  1. Season chicken thighs and add to oven at 375 degrees F for 14 minutes, flipping halfway
  2. Boil pot of water with a few pinches of salt, begin cooking pasta
  3. In a microwave safe bowl, add cottage cheese, cream cheese, cheese wedges, cheddar powder, ranch seasoning, and buffalo sauce. Mix, microwave for 45-60 seconds
  4. Remove, and blend until smooth and creamy
  5. Once pasta is done, drain, and let cool for ~1-2 minutes. If using same pot to mix, allow it to cool before adding ingredients
  6. Pour sauce over cooked pasta, and mix until pasta is fully coated with cheese sauce
  7. Remove cooked chicken from oven and chop into small pieces. Add mac n' cheese and mix until fully combined

Waffle McGriddle Sandwich

Description

Easy way to get your protein in!

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop of whey protein
  • 1 slice reduced fat provolone cheese
  • 1/2c egg whites
  • 3 tbspn sugar free syrup
  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice turkey bacon
  • 1/2 tspn baking powder

Directions

  1. Add protein, 1 egg, syrup, baking powder, and pinch of salt to bowl and mix
  2. Heat up your waffle pan. Once hot, add batter and cook
  3. Cook up your eggs and turkey bacon
  4. Assemble your sandwich!

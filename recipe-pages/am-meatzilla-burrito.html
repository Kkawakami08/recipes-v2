AM Meatzilla Burrito

Description

Low calorie and high protein breakfast burrito that is sure to satiate your savory needs!

Ingredients

  • Carb balance tortilla
  • 3 Slices Turkey Bacon
  • Turkey Sausage Patty
  • 150g liquid egg whites
  • Fat Free Cheddar Cheese

Directions

  1. Measure out 150g of egg whites into a bowl
  2. Add salt and pepper to your egg whites and stir
  3. Scramble your egg whites into a pan with nonstick spray
  4. Add 28g of fat free cheddar
  5. In the meantime, add your turkey sausage patty and bacon to the air fryer until crispy
  6. Warm up or slightly crisp your tortilla ebfore adding all ingredients
